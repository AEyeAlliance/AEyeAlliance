We are a group of four undergraduate female students in computer science who are currently partaking in the AI4SocialGood Lab at McGill University. Throughout the first 2 weeks of the lab, we learned about AI and machine learning techniques and are now applying this knowledge to solve a social problem. Using data acquired from rare book colelctions and the internet, we contructed our own dataset consisting of multiple sets of the Braille alphabet. We then trained an AI that will recognize these Braille characters and translate them into English. We plan to further develop this technology to be able to recognize words and eventually whole sentences. The main objective of this project is to bring awareness of Braille and to bridge the gap of communication between blind and non-blind people.
